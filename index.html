Our Story

RUHBER™ is a small independent company. We first opened our factories in 2017 in the great city of Boston, MA. This is where all of our products have been designed and created, right up until this day. RUHBER™ was founded by two great friends, Alex DeLarge and Travis Bickle. Alex and Travis began utilizing elastic bands in their efforts to solving their day-to-day problems. Fitting the broke college student stereotype, they were living on a tight budget and found elastic bands to be a cheap and effective tool. After finding many creative uses for the bands they decided to start a small company that would manufacture environmentally friendly elastic bands that are durable and strong. This is how RUHBER™ was born.
